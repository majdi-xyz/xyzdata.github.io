What We Do

Data Integration & Ingestion

Connect APIs, databases, SaaS platforms, and legacy systems into your modern data warehouse. We design resilient ingestion pipelines using tools like Fivetran, Airbyte, or custom solutions tailored to your needs.

ETL/ELT Pipeline Development

Build scalable, maintainable data transformation pipelines with dbt, Airflow, or your preferred orchestration tool. We focus on modularity, testing, and documentation to ensure long-term success.

Data Warehousing & Modeling

Design efficient data models optimized for analytics and BI. From dimensional modeling to data vault architectures, we structure your data for performance and flexibility.

Business Intelligence & Analytics

Build self-service analytics platforms with tools like Tableau, Looker, Power BI, or Metabase. Empower your teams to explore data and make informed decisions independently.

Data Quality & Observability

Implement data validation, testing, and monitoring frameworks to ensure accuracy and reliability. Catch issues before they impact business decisions using tools like Great Expectations and Monte Carlo.

AI/ML Enablement

Prepare your data infrastructure to support machine learning and AI initiatives. From feature stores to model deployment pipelines, we lay the foundation for advanced analytics.
